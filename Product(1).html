Cod fish



Cod is the common name for the demersal fish genus Gadus, belonging to the family Gadidae. It is an important source of vitamin A, vitamin D, vitamin E, and omega-3 fatty acids. Cod is also used as part of the common name for a number of other fish species, and one species that belongs to genus Gadus is commonly not called cod
